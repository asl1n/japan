Who are we?

Abroad Study Nepal Pvt. Ltd., established in 2015 and headquartered in Kathmandu, is a trusted educational consultancy committed to guiding Nepali students toward academic excellence in Japan. With nearly a decade of experience in the field of international education, we have built a reputation as a reliable partner for students seeking quality education abroad.

Our expertise spans across various aspects of international education systems, particularly Japan. Our mission is to simplify and personalize the study abroad journey by providing tailored, transparent, and ethical guidance to students and their families.

Over the years, we have successfully guided hundreds of students to prestigious institutions in Japan, helping them achieve their academic and career goals. What We Offer?

At Abroad Study Nepal Pvt. Ltd., we provide comprehensive, end-to-end support for Nepali students aspiring to pursue their education in Japan. Our process begins with personalized counseling sessions where we understand each student's academic background, career aspirations, and personal preferences. Based on this analysis, we guide them toward the most suitable institutions and programs that align with their ambitions. Our services include university and language school placements, student visa application assistance, Japanese language test preparation (JLPT, NAT, J.F), and document translation and verification. We also offer pre-departure briefings to help students prepare for life in Japan, covering cultural orientation, accommodation guidance, and practical tips for living abroad.

Additionally, we stay up to date on admissions, scholarships, and immigration policies.
