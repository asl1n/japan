Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for a Japanese student visa?

To apply for a Japanese student visa, you need a Certificate of Eligibility (COE), valid passport, completed visa application form, passport-sized photos, and proof of financial support.

Do I need to know Japanese to study in Japan?

While many programs are taught in English, basic Japanese knowledge is helpful for daily life. We recommend taking JLPT N5 or N4 level courses before departure.

What scholarships are available for international students?

There are various scholarships including MEXT, JASSO, and university-specific scholarships. We help students identify and apply for suitable scholarship opportunities.

How long can I stay after completing my studies?

After graduation, you can apply for a job-hunting visa that allows you to stay for up to 12 months to search for employment in Japan.

What accommodation options are available for students?

Students can choose from university dormitories, private apartments, share houses, or homestay programs. We assist in finding suitable accommodation based on your preferences and budget.

Why should we choose Japan for our further studies?

Japan offers world-class education, cutting-edge technology, rich culture, excellent career opportunities, and a safe environment for international students.
